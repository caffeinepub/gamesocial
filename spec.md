# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Populate the music library with songs and make podcasts accessible to users.

**Planned changes:**
- Add multiple songs to the backend music library with complete metadata (title, artist, album, duration)
- Add podcast content with episodes to the backend including metadata and audio references
- Display all songs in the music library page with functional play controls
- Display all podcasts in a grid layout with expandable episode lists that are playable

**User-visible outcome:** Users can browse and play a populated library of songs in the music page and discover/play podcasts with their episodes in the podcasts page.
